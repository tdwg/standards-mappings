Potential survey questions:

- existing/current mappings

  - that you already did

  - that you would like to do, but haven't managed yet

  - mapping to common standards, eg. DublinCore, IIIF, Schema.org

- Where does your mapping reside (location)

- How did you express the mapping? Which format is it in

- Which mapping standard do you follow? Do you know?

- How are you using mappings in data wrangling/how do you want to use them?

- Is the mapping in a machine-friendly/actionable form?

- How do you present mappings in a human-friendly presentation? Eg. if there are 1:n mappings of terms

- Which tools and or platforms are you using to a) work on mappings and b) to deposit mappings/as a repository.

- Would you be willing to present your mappings at a meeting of the TDWG Mappings TG?

- Confidence, age (year of mapping), quantification (completeness relative to whole standard vs. relative to usage/project need) -> might be best done as a description
