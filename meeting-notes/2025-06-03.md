## Draft for the E-Mail

Dear Standard-Maintainers and Collection-Management-System-Developers,

The TDWG Standards Mapping Task Group is conducting a survey to evaluate the existing mappings of the different TDWG standards.

Survey Link:[ https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScPx5q\_x4p8-14Eis9IviDbdKy0QSIdh-05F2huwoENtUmQVA/viewform](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScPx5q_x4p8-14Eis9IviDbdKy0QSIdh-05F2huwoENtUmQVA/viewform) (Deadline 15. July 2025)

If you are responsible for a standard or for creating mappings between standards and/or are using mappings, please fill out the survey for the mappings in question. If you have multiple mappings that you want to report on, you can combine them into one reply or fill out the survey multiple times, if they are significantly different. Whichever way you feel fits your situation best.

Feel free to spread the survey link to people who you think might be able to contribute to this survey. If you have any further questions, you can raise them in the Slack channel #mappings-between-standards in the[ TDWG slack space](https://www.tdwg.org/about/#slack), create an issue in the[ Github repository](https://github.com/tdwg/standards-mappings/issues) of the group, or email d.fichtmueller\@bgbm.org .

To learn more about the Standards Mapping Task Group, go to[ https://www.tdwg.org/about/committees/tag/standards-mapping/](https://www.tdwg.org/about/committees/tag/standards-mapping/) .

Thank you very much.

The TDWG Standards Mapping Task Group
